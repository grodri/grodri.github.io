This section has materials from Pop 507: Generalized Linear Statistical Models, as offered in the Fall of 2016 (the last time I taught this course). The course was cross-listed as WWS 509. The registrar's page for the course is here.

Syllabus

The 2016 syllabus is available in three parts:

  • A Course Description,
  • A List of Lectures, and
  • The list of Supplementary Readings.

A complete syllabus combining all three parts is also available in pdf format.

Links

Follow the tabs just below the banner to access:

  • The Lecture Notes, which are available online in formats suitable for browsing and printing,
  • The Stata Logs and R Logs, showing how to conduct the statistical analyses in the notes using Stata or R.
  • Copies of the small Datasets used in the course, including the program effort data. We also post here many of the datasets needed for the problem sets.
  • The Problem Sets, which will be made available for download during the term.
